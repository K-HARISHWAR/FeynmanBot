# FeynmanBot - Project Overview

## The Problem
Many students think they understand a topic after reading a textbook or watching a video, but when pressed for details, their knowledge falls apart. Traditional learning methods lack active recall and logical stress-testing.

## The Solution
FeynmanBot is an AI-powered education tool based on the Feynman Technique. Instead of the AI teaching the student, the **student teaches the AI**. By acting as a curious beginner, FeynmanBot asks targeted follow-up questions to expose logical gaps and misconceptions.

## Hackathon Pitch
"We built an AI that doesn't give you the answers—it forces you to find them. FeynmanBot turns passive learning into active teaching."
